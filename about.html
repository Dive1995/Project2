WHO WE ARE

The Inuvil Public Library is the main library and information centre in Inuvil. It intends to provide library resources as well as information to everyone. The library is in charge of a trained librarian. There are over 25000 books. Methods of acquiring library material are purchases, deposits, donations and exchange. Members of the library can borrow two books at a time and are expected to return them within seven days. members who damage, tear or mutilate books are fined.

There are two reading areas, one is outside the library and other one is inside the library .The daily newspaper and useful magazines are available in the reading room situated outside. Other reading stuffs are available inside the library. A collection of historical books, a large selection of magazine and newspapers are available in the library.There is a children library. It has more than 1000 books and magazines suitable for school children. There is a leisure hall ,computer lab and study hall. This library building was completed and officially opened on 2013. Inuvil public library's IT center is called Nenasala. In here they teach basic computer course for charge. There is also a chidrens' park adjoining this public library.

BACKGROUND

Inuvil is a small town in the northern Srilanka. The town surrounding the surrounding area has traditionally been populated by farmers. Inuvil library is a prime source of Inuvil. Inuvil library has a long history of library service. It is one of the prime source of northern information and a hub of community activity.

Inuvil library was established in 1948.in the early times it was known as SRI GANESSHA VASIGASALAI. Inuvil library has a long history of library service. It is well-stocked with all the books and reference books. At start books were donated by wellwishers.
